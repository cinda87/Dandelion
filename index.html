Meet Dandelion: My POTS Service Dog

Dandelion is not just a pet—she’s my dedicated service dog, trained to assist me with managing the daily challenges of living with Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome (POTS). POTS is a condition that affects my autonomic nervous system, causing dizziness, fatigue, and heart rate abnormalities, especially when standing. Dandelion plays a vital role in helping me navigate the world more independently and safely. With her calm demeanor and intuitive understanding, Dandelion provides invaluable support by alerting me to signs of instability and offering balance when I need it most. Whether she’s guiding me through a crowd, providing comfort during moments of weakness, or just being my constant companion, Dandelion has become an irreplaceable part of my life. Her presence brings not only physical assistance but also emotional support, reminding me that I’m never alone in my journey with POTS.
